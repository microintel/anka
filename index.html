About Anka

How it works:

  1. Add a stage — tap Add and pick a type (SSLC, Diploma, Engineering, PUC, Medical, ITI, or Custom), then choose annual or semester-wise.
  2. Enter your marks — open the stage and add subjects with internal/external marks; Anka works out totals, percentages and grades automatically.
  3. Track your progress — check Statistics and Charts anytime to see trends, compare stages, and reorder them however you like.
  4. Sign in to sync — from Account, sign in with Google or email/password so your data can travel with you across devices.
  5. Back up to the cloud — tap Backup to Cloud to save everything to your account; Restore from Cloud pulls it back down on any device you sign into. Each backup fully replaces the previous cloud copy, so make sure this device has your latest edits before backing up — especially if you use Anka on more than one device.
  6. Export, print, or reset — use Export or PDF Report to keep an offline copy, or Factory Reset to wipe this device's local data without touching your cloud backup.

Why Anka exists: report cards, mark sheets, and grade slips pile up across years and get lost between phones, folders, and forgotten drawers. Anka was built so a student never has to dig through old photos or paperwork to answer a simple question — "what were my marks in that subject, that semester?" One record, from your first stage to your last, always at hand.
