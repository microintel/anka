About Anka

Most students end up with their marks scattered everywhere — a photo of one marksheet, a PDF of another, a few scribbled in a notebook. Anka was built to fix that: one place to keep every mark from every stage of your education, from SSLC to your final semester, so nothing ever gets lost.

How to use it: tap Add to create a stage (SSLC, Diploma, Engineering, PUC, Medical, ITI, or your own custom one) and choose whether it's annual or semester-wise. Open that stage and add your subjects with internal/external marks — Anka calculates totals, percentages and grades automatically. Check Statistics anytime to see charts of your progress, compare stages, and reorder them the way you like. Use Export or the PDF Report button to back up or share your full record.
